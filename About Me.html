Prompt Engineer

I specialize in designing, structuring, and optimizing prompts that enable AI systems to produce accurate, context-aware, and high-quality outputs. My work focuses on translating complex human intent into clear, well-defined instructions that guide large language models toward reliable reasoning, creativity, and task execution. I approach prompt engineering as both a technical and creative discipline. On the technical side, I apply structured prompting techniques such as role assignment, step-by-step reasoning, constraint definition, and output formatting to improve consistency, precision, and usability of AI responses. On the creative side, I experiment with tone, perspective, and abstraction to unlock innovative outputs for content creation, ideation, and design support. My experience includes building prompts for:

  • Content generation (articles, captions, scripts, summaries)
  • Code assistance and debugging workflows
  • /UX ideation and user-centric design exploration
  • Research, analysis, and information synthesis
  • Automation of repetitive tasks and productivity optimization
